Biography:


Growing up in a small rural town, my favorite pastime during the rainy months was baking cupcakes with my mom. Every Sunday we'd go door to door and give cupcakes to our neighbors. Everyone loved them! Once I got to college, to make some extra cash, I started selling cupcakes to friends and floormates for birthdays and parties. That small taste of running a business gave me the freedom and flexibility to pursue my passion while studying, and I realized that what I really wanted to do with my career was make delicious treats and bring people smiles with my cupcakes! After I graduated, I decided to take the plunge and create my cupcake business. Even though it was painful to leave the security of my field of study, I am forever grateful that I did, because it led to a life and business I love! Five years later and I am now the proud owner of an award-winning cupcake shop, Takes the Cake. We've even been featured on Cupcake Wars! Now, I want to give back the community and show women how empowering and exciting it can be to start their own food business and start cooking up their tastiest futures!
